Founder & Director

Vinay Garg

Vinay Garg is the Founder & Director of Garg Enterprises, Bathinda. With over 21 years of experience in the electronics and home appliances industry, Vinay Garg previously worked with leading brands including Whirlpool, LG, Sharp, and IFB Industries, where he served as Business Manager.

Drawing on his deep industry expertise, Vinay Garg founded Garg Enterprises to bring professional-grade product knowledge, genuine brands, and transparent pricing directly to customers in Bathinda. The showroom is built on trusted after-sales support and a commitment to helping every customer make the right purchase decision.
